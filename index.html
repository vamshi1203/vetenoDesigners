About Us

We're a team of five friends who've been passionate about design and architecture since our student days. Our bond, forged over years of collaboration and shared experiences, has been the cornerstone of our journey. After gaining valuable experience in various companies, we realized that our shared vision and complementary skills could create something truly unique. Together, we founded Veteno Designers in 2019, with the goal of transforming spaces into personalized havens. Our team's expertise is further enriched by the presence of our founder, D.Sohan, a renowned chief architect and designer who played a pivotal role in the prestigious Yadadri temple project. His legacy of excellence and innovation inspires us to strive for the highest standards in every project. Since our inception, we've been committed to crafting interiors that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and comfortable. We believe that a well-designed space should reflect the personality and lifestyle of its occupants. Our journey has been marked by successful projects, satisfied clients, and a growing reputation. We've leveraged social media platforms, such as Instagram, to showcase our work and connect with potential clients. At Veteno Designers, we're more than just designers; we're partners in your vision. We're dedicated to understanding your unique needs and preferences to create a space that truly feels like home.
"Let's collaborate to transform your space into a reflection of your dreams."
